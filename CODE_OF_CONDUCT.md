# Code of Conduct

## Commitment

Fenstream Technology Group is committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free community.

## Expected Behavior

- communicate professionally and constructively
- collaborate in good faith
- provide actionable, respectful feedback
- focus on technical issues, not personal attacks

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
- abusive or threatening language
- doxxing or privacy violations
- sustained disruption of project collaboration spaces

## Scope

Applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and official communication channels.

## Reporting

Report concerns to: opensource_conduct@fenstreamtech.group

Provide, where possible:

- what occurred
- links/evidence
- date/time and participants

## Enforcement

Maintainers and designated moderators may apply proportionate actions, including warnings, content removal, temporary restrictions, or permanent bans.
