# Governance

## Ownership

Repositories under this baseline are governed by Fenstream Technology Group Ltd maintainers and designated policy owners.

## Roles

- Maintainers: review/merge PRs, manage releases, enforce repository policy
- Contributors: propose and implement changes through standard workflow
- Security Contacts: coordinate vulnerability response and disclosure
- Policy Owners: maintain and approve baseline policy updates and exceptions

## Decision Process

- routine changes: maintainer consensus
- significant changes: documented proposal and review period
- security/legal matters: security contacts + policy owners

## Release Authority

Maintainers control release readiness, timing, and changelog publication.

## Policy Hierarchy

1. Applicable law/regulatory requirement
2. Approved repository-level exception
3. Group baseline policy files

Where conflicts exist, the stricter control applies unless legal obligations require otherwise.
